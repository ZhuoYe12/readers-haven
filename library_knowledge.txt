=== LIBRARY FAQ ===

Q: What are the library hours?
A: Our digital library is available 24/7 online! You can browse, borrow, and reserve books anytime.

Q: How many books can I borrow?
A: You can borrow up to 5 books at a time for 14 days each.

Q: Can I renew a borrowed book?
A: Yes! You can renew up to 3 times if no one has reserved it.

Q: What if I return a book late?
A: There's a $0.50 per day late fee for overdue books.

Q: How do I reserve a book?
A: If a book is currently unavailable, click the "Reserve" button. You'll be notified when it's available and have 7 days to pick it up.

Q: Can I see my borrowed books?
A: Yes! Click your name in the top-right corner and select "My Borrowed Books" to see all your active borrows, due dates, and return options.

Q: How do I return a book?
A: Go to "My Borrowed Books" and click the "Return Book" button next to the book you want to return.

Q: Is there a mobile app?
A: The website is fully responsive and works perfectly on mobile browsers!
